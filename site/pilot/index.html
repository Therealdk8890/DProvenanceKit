Clear boundaries

DProvenanceKit compares the observable, instrumented execution path. It does not expose hidden chain-of-thought, guarantee factual correctness, detect every possible AI failure, or provide a blanket compliance certification.

The hosted/team layer is currently available through manually provisioned pilots and design partnerships. This pilot does not promise a fully self-service SaaS product or production uptime SLA.
